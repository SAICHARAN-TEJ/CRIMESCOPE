What We Actually Built

CrimeScope is a swarm intelligence visualization layer on top of the OASIS multi-agent simulation engine. We took hundreds of autonomous agents — each with independent personality profiles, long-term memory, and behavioral logic — and gave investigators a way to see them all at once. Every node is an entity. Every edge is a proven relationship. Every click reveals the intelligence underneath.
